**Client:** James Halvorson, a friend and fitness enthusiast who is interested in helping individuals simplify healthy eating and meal preparation while staying within a budget. 

**Purpose:** To provide users with personalized meal prep recipes and affordable grocery shopping lists based on their fitness goals, dietary needs, and budget. 

**Audience:** The primary target audience is college students and young adults who are interested in fitness, muscle gain, weight loss, or maintaining a healthy lifestyle. Many individuals in this demographic have limited time, cooking experience, and financial resources, making it difficult to consistently prepare nutritious meals. The platform aims to make healthy eating more accessible, affordable, and convenient. 

**Key action:** Users will enter information such as their fitness goals, calorie targets, macronutrient preferences, dietary restrictions, food preferences, and grocery budget. The platform will then generate customized recipes, provide calorie and macronutrient breakdowns, and create a personalized grocery shopping list with estimated prices to help users stay on track with their nutrition and fitness goals.
