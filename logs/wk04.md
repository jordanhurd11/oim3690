- **What AI gave me**: 
Great question! The user inputs are saved to your browser's **localStorage** (not sent to any server). Here are a few ways to view them:

### Option 1: Via Browser DevTools (quickest)

1. Open your index.html in a browser
2. Press **F12** or **Ctrl+Shift+I** to open DevTools
3. Go to the **Application** tab (Chrome/Edge) or **Storage** tab (Firefox)
4. In the left sidebar, expand **Local Storage** and select your page
5. You'll see a key called `fuelPrepUserInputs` — double-click it to see the saved JSON with goal, calories, restrictions, budget, and timestamp

### Option 2: Via the Console

In DevTools, go to the **Console** tab and type:

```js
JSON.parse(localStorage.getItem("fuelPrepUserInputs"))
```

Hit Enter and it'll print the saved object.
